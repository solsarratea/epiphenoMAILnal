

EPISODE 6: You and your email
---------------------------------------------------------------

Let's give us a self-reflection time and think about our history on the internet. Email may had been a fundamental part. And for many of us led us to the initiation of virtual communication. But for contextual reasons happening around it, the experience of email has changed, but it still your passport in this virtual world.

Possible questions:
- How did you feel and how DO you feel when you are checking your inbox?
- How has our experience been transformed?
- WHAT DID WE DO TO YOU?
